All There is to Know About The COVID-19 Vaccine

March 16th, 2021

There are quite a few COVID-19 vaccines released at the time of writing this article. Some examples include Pfizer’s vaccine, Moderna’s vaccine, and Johnson & Johnson’s. This article will answer frequently asked questions and concerns regarding these vaccines.


Are the vaccines safe?

Yes! All vaccines go through vigorous testing before being released to the public.


How old do you have to be to get the vaccine?

Over 16 years old. There are no COVID-19 vacines approved for youth younger than 16 years old at the timing of writing this article.


How does the vaccine work?

Vaccines help the body recognize and attack threats before the threats can take action before it's too late. For more infomation, read the article linked below.


Do I still need to social distance and wear a mask after getting the vaccine?

Yes! The vaccines an average of 95% chance of working, which means you may not necessarily be immune to the virus after vaccination. Also, in respect for others, it is still best to socially distance to avoid the possibility transmitting the virus to someone unvaccinated.

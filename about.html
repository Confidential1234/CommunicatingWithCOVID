So what is Telecommunications?
Throughout history, people have desired to communicate both faster and farther. With the onset of electronical transmissions and computers, health services are also evolving to meet the fast-paced, digital world of today. Telecommunications is the process of communicating through cables, phones, or broadcasting. Basically, it is just virtually sending and receiving information without having physical interactions.
